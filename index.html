Our Campus

A peaceful, green retreat set among palms and gardens at Charalkunnu.

The Christian Education Centre, Charalkunnu is a spacious campus designed for conferences, camps, and spiritual retreats. Set amid landscaped gardens and mature palm groves, it offers a calm, welcoming environment for groups of every size.

Facilities

Choose the space that fits your programme.

Entire Campus

Education Centre

A dedicated building suited for classes, seminars, and mid-sized gatherings.

Thejus

A separate hall/facility, ideal for smaller programmes and meetings.

Rooms

A/C, Non A/C, and Dormitory accommodation (with separate Male/Female dormitory space).
